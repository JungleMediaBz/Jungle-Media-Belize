BABOON SANCTUARY
TOURS
Nestled in the lush greenery of Belize, the Baboon Sanctuary is a haven for wildlife and a testament to harmonious coexistence between humans and nature. Spanning over 20 square miles, this remarkable sanctuary is home to the charismatic black howler monkeys, locally known as "baboons." With their resonant calls echoing through the rainforest, these creatures embody the vibrant spirit of the Belizean wilderness.

BELIZE ZOO
TOURS
The Belize Zoo, often called "The Best Little Zoo in the World," is a unique and heartwarming wildlife sanctuary that showcases the incredible diversity of Belize's native animals. Established in 1983, the zoo is home to over 175 animals, all of which are rescues, orphans, or born at the zoo

ANCIENT MAYA
HISTORY
Step back in time and explore the remnants of one of the world's greatest ancient civilizations. Belize is home to numerous Maya archaeological sites, each revealing fascinating insights into the rich history and culture of this enigmatic people. From the towering temples of Caracol to the intricately carved stones of Xunantunich, these ancient ruins offer a glimpse into the past and a connection to the spiritual and astronomical knowledge of the Maya.
