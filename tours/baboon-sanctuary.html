Baboon Sanctuary

Nestled in the lush greenery of Belize, the Baboon Sanctuary is a haven for wildlife and a testament to harmonious coexistence between humans and nature. Spanning over 20 square miles, this remarkable sanctuary is home to the charismatic black howler monkeys, locally known as "baboons."

Tour Highlights

  • Observe black howler monkeys in their natural habitat
  • Guided nature walks through the sanctuary
  • Learn about local conservation efforts
  • Experience traditional Belizean village life
  • Bird watching opportunities
